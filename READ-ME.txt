The sound file "sounds.txt" in ".\Air Guitar Framework\files\guitar\sounds" sets the possible guitar sounds and the playback used song.

The camera file "camera.xml" in ".\Air Guitar Framework\files\camera" sets the current capture mode, changing between webcam capture and video file input by just alternating the part commented in the file.

To find more details about the work please look for the paper at
http://www.sbgames.org/papers/sbgames09/computing/full/cp9_09.pdf
